# Do You See Me Now

Fabric mod for Minecraft 1.20.1 that reworks how mobs detect players.

Vanilla mobs have 360° awareness and instantly know where you are the moment you're in range. This mod changes that.

## What it does

- **Field of view** — Mobs can only see within 120° in front of them. Sneak up from behind and they won't notice.
- **Light matters** — Detection range scales with the light level at the player's position. Bright = far, dark = close. Torches are a tradeoff now.
- **Investigation** — Hit a mob from the shadows and it won't instantly lock onto you. It walks to where it thinks the attack came from and looks around.
- **Visual feedback** — A yellow **!** when a mob spots you, a blue **?** when it's searching.

This is a work in progress. Mechanics and values are subject to change.
